Comments: MSE will first decrease when $\lambda$ increases, while reach the lowest value when $\lambda=2.153395$, and then increase with the increase of $\lambda$. It is because when $\lambda$ is too small, then it will lead to lots of predictors being selected, which increase the Overfitting, the MSE large. With the increase of $\lambda$, the penalty of selecting more variables increases, so model is going to perform better till the best $\lambda$. At the best $\lambda$, there is the best trade-off of the Overfitting and Underfitting, with a relatively rational number of variables. After that, due to the high penalty, there will be much less variables selected resulting in Underfitting.
